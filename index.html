Who are we!

Kingdom Paints is a division of Adelogue Holdings, a limited company incorporated in Zimbabwe under the Company’s Act (Chapter 24:03). The Company opened its doors to business on 1 June 2009 bringing together in the process well known personalities with requisite expertise in the paint industry. The company brought at its formation professionals in technical, marketing and managerial experience spanning over many years in its promoters and other support staff collectively.
